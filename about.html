I am a designer and front-end developer from Columbus, OH.

Web Development

I stumbled upon basic HTML and CSS tutorials during my early undergrad years, and consequently fell in love with with the potential of not only being able to visualize my ideas but also develop them from the ground up on my own as well.

Out of this interest for development, my passion for functionality and structure evolved. Jumping right into Photoshop to create something didn’t feel accurate anymore and skipping the steps of building a framework based on functionality and usability became inevitable.

Design

A user-centered mindset and awareness for design turned out to be the perfect fit for me whilst creating on my own as well as when collaborating with others. This rough framework outlines my process of developing digital experiences:

  1. RESEARCH and gather the starting point and evaluate requirements
  2. STRUCTURE setup and content of the project
  3. CONCEPT AND STRATEGY
